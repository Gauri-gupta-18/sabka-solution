How It Works

A transparent, step-by-step process from reporting to resolution.

1. Report an Issue

Snap a photo of the problem (pothole, garbage, broken streetlight) and add a brief description along with the location.

2. AI Analysis

Our AI engine automatically detects the category, severity, and checks for duplicates, ensuring efficient routing.

3. Community Verification

Local citizens confirm the issue to build trust. Highly verified issues get prioritized automatically.

4. Authority Action

The assigned department receives the verified report and begins working on a resolution.

5. Resolution & Impact

Once resolved, you receive a notification, and you earn Trust Points for contributing to your city.

Your voice. Our community. One solution. Empowering citizens and authorities to build better cities together through AI and community collaboration.
